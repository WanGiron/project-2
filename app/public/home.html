Welcome To Little Feet

At Little feet, we recognize that a child’s early years of development are the foundational building blocks that prepare them for a lifetime love of learning. Children naturally grow and learn through play, and we help foster that exploration in a safe, secure, and nurturing environment. Our experienced staff understands that each child has their own unique style of learning and are able to adjust their lesson plans to accommodate each child’s needs. We help foster your child’s individuality and by engaging them in various fun, educational, and age appropriate activities.
